About Me

I realized my passion for data while completing my Master's Degree in Crminal Justice and Public saftey. A course in ArcGIS as well as a statistics course introduced me to more advanced data manipulation and analysis than I had been previously exposed. I enjoyed it much more than I had anticipated and began wishing I had chosen the tech field from the beginning. Now armed with a Bachelor's in Psychology and a Master's in Criminal Justice, I worried that despite my love of education, this would not be enough to break into tech. I had applied for several Data Analyst positions within the government to no avail.


My husband, a software developer of 6 years, encouraged me to start learning software development. Through the encouragement of his co-workers, I enrolled in a Software Development Bootcamp with ElevenFifty Academy. Though the bootcamp was intense and challenging, I enjoyed every moment (even the "so frustrated I want to throw my computer out the window" moments). I found that I could utilize my love of learning, research, and problem solving to a high degree with Software Development given the many different ways of addressing any problem or challenge. I have been more highly engaged with the work I have been doing and have found a greater sense of accomplishment with each little success or discovery.
